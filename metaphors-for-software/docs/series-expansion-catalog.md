# Series Expansion Catalog

Metaphors for Software expansion candidates, grouped into coherent tracks with like-minded pairings for curriculum bundles, launch waves, and cross-promotion.

## Positioning Rules

- Preserve the Rule of Three for every candidate title.
- Keep each title anchored to one dominant metaphor.
- Avoid duplicate scope between sibling books in the same launch wave.
- Pair each advanced title with one beginner-friendly companion.

## Candidate Tracks

| Track | Focus | Candidate Titles |
|---|---|---|
| Workshop Foundations 2.0 | Core CS intuition and JavaScript fluency | Kindergarten DSA, D+S+A, Yes JS! |
| City Networking Deep Dive | Addressing, routing, and topology architecture | The Subnetting Toy Box, The Route Map, The Lay of the Land |
| Workshop Async Kitchen | Asynchronous execution and promise workflows | Sync with Asynchronous Programming, The Promises Chef |

## Like-Minded Clusters

- The Subnetting Toy Box
- The Route Map
- The Lay of the Land
- Existing anchor: Subnetting Espresso

### Cluster B: Async Flow Suite
