    Part 1: The Metaphorical Story

    The Story of the Builder Who Measured Every Box

    A neighborhood school receives one giant toy chest. It works for a week, then chaos starts: puzzle pieces mix with blocks, science kits disappear, and game cards spill into every corner. The caretaker, Mara, does not buy a bigger chest. She installs sliding walls.

    Each wall sets a clear boundary. Each section gets a label. She keeps one label for the section itself, one reserved card no child can claim, and one final card to announce cleanup time across the whole section.

    The breakthrough comes when a new class arrives and needs a tiny section just for partner games. Mara does not rebuild everything. She slices one section into smaller boxes while preserving the main labels.

    By the end, every class knows exactly where play begins, where it ends, and which door to use when toys need to move between rooms.

    Appendix A: Lab Solutions

    Lab 13-01

    Hint

    Cut one /24 in half by borrowing one host bit.

    Step by Step

    1. Borrow one bit from host space: /24 becomes /25.
    2. Block size is 128 in last octet.
    3. Subnets are .0/25 and .128/25.

    Full Solution

    10.20.0.0/25   usable: 10.20.0.1   to 10.20.0.126
10.20.0.128/25 usable: 10.20.0.129 to 10.20.0.254

    Lab 13-02

    Hint

    Allocate largest subnet first, then continue descending by need.

    Step by Step

    1. 50 hosts requires /26.
    2. 20 hosts requires /27.
    3. Each point-to-point link requires /30.

    Full Solution

    Start with 10.20.1.0/24
- Team A (50 hosts): 10.20.1.0/26
- Team B (20 hosts): 10.20.1.64/27
- P2P-1: 10.20.1.96/30
- P2P-2: 10.20.1.100/30

    Lab 13-03

    Hint

    Sort ranges by network start and compare end boundaries for overlap.

    Step by Step

    1. Convert each CIDR to numeric start and end.
    2. Check if next start falls within previous range.
    3. Move conflicting subnet to first available non-overlapping block.

    Full Solution

    Broken plan:
- 10.30.0.0/26
- 10.30.0.32/27   (overlaps)

Corrected:
- 10.30.0.0/26
- 10.30.0.64/27
