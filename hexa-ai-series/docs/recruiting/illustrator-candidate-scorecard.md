## Risk Flags

Mark any that apply.

- [ ] Style is attractive but inconsistent across samples
- [ ] Strong single-image artist, weak sequential storytelling
- [ ] Limited children's audience fit
- [ ] Limited production or print readiness
- [ ] Slow or unclear communication during application process
- [ ] Rates outside budget range without clear justification
- [ ] Availability does not match expected schedule
- [ ] Low comfort with revisions or collaboration
- [ ] Low comfort with scientific subject matter

## Suggested Thresholds

- 40+ portfolio score: strong candidate worth interviewing
- 32-39 portfolio score: possible candidate if style fit is unusually strong
- Under 32: do not advance unless there is a specific strategic reason
- 70+ total score: strong hire candidate
- 60-69 total score: viable if risks are manageable
- Under 60 total score: do not hire for lead series role
